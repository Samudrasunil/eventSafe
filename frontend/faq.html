Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is EventSafe?

EventSafe is a platform designed to help event organizers manage emergencies and provide safety features like a panic button.

How can I use the Panic Button?

Simply click the panic button in the app when you're in an emergency, and it will send an alert with your live location.

How do I register for an event?

You can register for an event by filling out the registration form on the Event Registration page.
